02 / 03 — Healthcare

Patient-Centric Healthcare Center

Consultation Room
Washroom
CT Scan Suite

A modern healthcare center designed around patient comfort — a welcoming reception and waiting area, consultation room, CT scan suite, and washroom. The brief called for efficient space planning and seamless circulation, but the real challenge was making a clinical program feel calm: natural materials, soft geometry, and warm lighting throughout, without compromising the operational needs of a working medical facility.

Scope
Reception & Waiting Area · Consultation Room · CT Scan Suite · Washroom
Materials
Light ash wood panelling Soft-touch vinyl flooring Frosted glass partitions
Lighting
Diffused ceiling wash + warm accent lighting to offset clinical fixtures, calming rather than sterile
Tools
Blender / CyclesPhotoshop
